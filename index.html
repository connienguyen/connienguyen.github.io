Gothenburg Underground Tour

12-22 April 2018

Gothenburg Underground Tour is an audio-visual guide to four sites in Gothenburg with a hidden underground. As a tour participant you are invited to go to any or all of these sites in the order you choose. There you will find an augmented reality ‘marker’, which you can activate on a smartphone using the AR camera link. If prompted, allow access to your camera view, then point at the marker to view the augmented reality.

Locations

Änggårdsbergen

Marker location*
Walk through the botanical gardens and up the staircase to Änggårdsbergen nature reserve. Markers can be found on the roofs of the military bunkers.

Nearest stop
Göteborg Botaniska Trädgården

Free Wi-Fi
Please note there is no Wi-Fi here use 3g or download mp3 before you go!

Lilla Bommen

Marker location*
Inside pedestrian overpass tunnel connecting nordstan shopping centre to lilla bommen, behind the handrail directly above the göta tunneln underground car tunnel.

Nearest stop
Lilla Bommen/ Nordstan

Free Wi-Fi
_Gothenburg WiFi

Linnéstaden

Marker location*
On the ground at transport stand B

Nearest stop
Linnéplatsen

Free Wi-Fi
GothenburgGuest

Please note the Wi-Fi connection can be lost during guided movemement on this tour. Use 3g or download mp3 before you go!

Masthugget

Marker location*
Go towards the shops and up the staircase or elevator. The marker is located on the ground nearby skogen arts centre.

Nearest stop
Masthugget

Free Wi-Fi
GothenburgGuest

* Can't find the marker here? You can scan the markers above for each site instead!
